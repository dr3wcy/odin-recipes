Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • cheese
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Noodles
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Step 1: Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Step 2: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
  3. Step 3: Make the cheese layer, mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Step 4: Assemble the lasagna, layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  5. Step 5: Bake the lasagna, layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
